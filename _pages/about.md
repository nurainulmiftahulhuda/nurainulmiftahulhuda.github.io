## Welcome

Welcome to my academic website.

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Mathematics, Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences, Universitas Tanjungpura, Indonesia. My research focuses on **spatio-temporal statistics**, **time series analysis**, **Generalized Space-Time Autoregressive (GSTAR) models**, **dynamic spatial dependence**, and **environmental statistical modelling**.

My recent work aims to develop advanced statistical methodologies for modelling complex environmental phenomena, particularly forest fires, climate-related hazards, and spatial risk prediction using satellite observations, meteorological variables, and Internet of Things (IoT) data.
